About Oregon Tires Auto Care

Family-owned. Community-driven. Serving Portland since 2008.

Our Story

Oregon Tires Auto Care was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: provide Portland families with honest, reliable automotive services at fair prices. What began as a small tire shop on SE 82nd Avenue has grown into a full-service auto care center trusted by thousands of customers across the Portland metro area.

As a family-owned business, we treat every customer like family. We believe in transparency -- no hidden fees, no unnecessary upsells, just honest work at honest prices. Our team takes pride in explaining what your vehicle needs and why, so you can make informed decisions about your car care.

Over the years, we have built lasting relationships with our customers. Many of the families we served back in 2008 still trust us with their vehicles today -- and that is the kind of loyalty that drives everything we do.

Our Vision

To be Portland's most trusted auto care provider -- a place where every driver, regardless of background or language, feels welcomed, respected, and confident in the service they receive. We envision a community where quality automotive care is accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Mission

To deliver professional automotive services with integrity, transparency, and care. We are committed to using quality parts, providing honest assessments, and offering bilingual support so that every customer in our diverse community can communicate comfortably about their vehicle needs.
