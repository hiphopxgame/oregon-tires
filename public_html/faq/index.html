Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, hours, and more.

Oregon Tires offers a full range of automotive services including new and used tires, tire installation and repair, oil changes (conventional, synthetic, high-mileage), brake services, wheel alignment (2-wheel and 4-wheel), engine diagnostics, suspension repair, tuneups, mechanical inspections, mobile service, and roadside assistance. Visit our Services page for full details and pricing.

Yes! Oregon Tires has a fully bilingual staff fluent in both English and Spanish. We are proud to serve Portland's diverse community in the language you are most comfortable with. Our website is also available in Spanish -- just click the language toggle at the top of any page.

Oregon Tires is open Monday through Saturday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. For emergency roadside assistance outside of normal hours, please call (503) 367-9714.

Oregon Tires is located at 8536 SE 82nd Ave, Portland, OR 97266. We are conveniently located on SE 82nd Avenue, easily accessible from I-205 and serving the greater Portland metro area including Clackamas, Milwaukie, and Happy Valley.

Yes, Oregon Tires offers mobile service at your home or any location. Whether you need tire installation, repair, or roadside assistance, we can come to you. Mobile service is available throughout the Portland metro area. Call (503) 367-9714 to schedule.

You can book an appointment online through our online booking page, or call us directly at (503) 367-9714. When you book online, our team will confirm your appointment by phone or text within a few hours. Walk-ins are also welcome -- no appointment necessary for many services.

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), and debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service. We provide detailed invoices for all work performed.

Yes, we offer both new and quality used tires. Our used tires are carefully inspected to ensure they meet safety standards and still have plenty of tread life. Used tires are a great budget-friendly option while still providing reliable performance. We can help you choose the best option for your needs and budget.

A standard tire installation typically takes 30-45 minutes for a set of four tires, including mount and balance. Single tire installs or repairs can be done in as little as 15-20 minutes. Wait times may vary based on current shop volume, but most tire services are completed while you wait.

Yes, we stand behind our work. New tires come with manufacturer warranties, and our repair services include a workmanship guarantee. If you experience any issues after a repair, bring your vehicle back and we will make it right. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.
